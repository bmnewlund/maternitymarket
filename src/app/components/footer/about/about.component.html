Maternity market

The Pascascios, an Indianapolis family, is raising a baby for the first time. They're finding themselves, all of a sudden, in great need of items that they would likely never need otherwise: Strollers, cribs, clothes, toys, etc.

Elsewhere in Indianapolis, the O'Connor family has a baby who has outgrown their stroller, crib, clothes, and toys.

At Maternity Market, the Pascascios can find everything they need for their family at a more comfortable cost than they'd find buying new. At the same time, the O'Connors are able to make space in their home while making a few deposits in their bank account.

Whenever the Pascascio family is ready, they can create a Maternity Market account a seller and help out a brand new Indianapolis family: the Jacksons.
